Use of Javascript and Leaflet Depicting Sequential Homicide Data

View this visual here, or click the map below

About

This visual depicts changes in homicide victims per 100,000 people in 15 different countries from 2014 to 2020, in response to the Lab 1 assignment.

Throughout my studies in Madison, the phenomenon of crime and its related geography has always interested me. Previously, I have examined the relationship between crime and green space.

Here, I wanted to look at crime from a global perspective, and assess similarities and differences across regions. This map enables users to analyze relationships and see where homicides are increasing and/or proportionately significant, while also exhibiting adequate use of javascript.

Data extracted from http://data.un.org/DocumentData.aspx?q=crime&id=443
